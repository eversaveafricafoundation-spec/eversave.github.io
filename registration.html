EVER-SAVE AFRICA FOUNDATION 

Energy Management Support for GES Staff.

As part of our commitment to support Ghana’s educators, Ever-Save Africa Foundation is offering a special relief package exclusively for GES staff. With this initiative, all installation and registration fees have been fully covered by Ever-Save Africa—meaning you enjoy the service completely free.

REGISTER NOW !

This initiative is meant to provide free access to the world class nanotechnological service provided by Kelandrews Energy Solutions Ltd. Installations are done by Energy Commissions Certified Electricians.
Anyone one who does not work under GES can still access the service provided by Kelandrews by paying a registration free and also installation fee, and this is done by directly contacting them via kelandrews.com

What you need to know !

BENEFITS

REDUCES YOUR ELECTRICITY BILLS BY 30% OR MORE 

The nano technology used in the propriety service rendered by The Kelandrews Energy Solutions Ltd. is one which is able to prevent loss of electrical energy through heat and EMR. This accounts for close to 40% of our energy bills. Therefore after successful installation, energy bills drop by 30% or more.

PROTECT YOUR LIFE AND PROPERTY

Before an installation is done, safety checks are done in compliance with the constitutional LI (L.I. 2008). Making sure that the electrical system is safe and reaches the constitutional standard of Ghana

SAVE  30% ON FUEL CONSUMPTION

The tech guarantees next level refining in the fuel used in vehicles; petrol diesel, and LPG. This enables the engine to burn it efficiently and leading to a reduction is consumption and better MpG.

IMPROVES ENGINE PERFORMANCE AND ENGINE LIFESPAN

Engines fed with further refined fuels have better lifespan than those that run on otherwise.

REDUCES EMISSIONS 

A significant benefit of this installation goes to the universe, ie. reduction in the emission of Carbon mono-oxide and other toxins as byproducts from the combustion  process making the planet a safer place.
