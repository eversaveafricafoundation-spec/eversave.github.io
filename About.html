Mission Statement

Ever-Save Africa is dedicated to raising awareness about the dangers of electromagnetic radiation and promoting energy conservation to enhance health and reduce poverty

SUPPORT Us

Vision

To foster a continent where communities are empowered with knowledge and resources to protect themselves from electromagnetic radiation and to conserve energy, promoting a healthier, sustainable, and economically vibrant Africa.

What We Do

We Care About Humanity

Radiation Protection Advocacy

At Ever-Save Africa, we are committed to raising awareness about the dangers of electromagnetic radiation and promoting safe and sustainable technology use.

Energy Conservation Advocacy

Ever-Save Africa is dedicated to promoting energy conservation, highlighting its significant financial and environmental benefits.

Public Education Initiatives

Public education is a cornerstone of Ever-Save Africa's mission. We engage communities through seminars, school visits, and corporate workshops to educate them on very important issues. 

Water for Health Project

At ​Ever-Save Africa, we partner with industry players in order to help people access clean water and also leverage on if for good health.
